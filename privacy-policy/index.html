Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LucyLP.com handles information for visitors, readers, and collectors.

LucyLP.com

LucyLP.com is a digital destination for vinyl collectors, collector stories, music history, and the LucyLP Music Press digital magazine.

Information We Collect

LucyLP does not ask visitors to create an account and does not collect unnecessary personal data through the public website. The collector value form asks for record details such as artist, album, pressing, and condition so the site can return marketplace-style results.

The LucyLP Collector List form currently uses a temporary mailto fallback to request newsletter signup. LucyLP does not store newsletter email addresses in frontend JavaScript or public website files.

Cookies And Analytics

LucyLP currently does not use tracking cookies.

Our current public pages do not include analytics scripts, tracking pixels, embedded advertising trackers, localStorage, or sessionStorage for visitor tracking.

Digital Magazine

LucyLP Music Press is available to read online and download as a PDF. Reading or downloading the magazine does not require personal information from visitors.

Affiliate Links

LucyLP may display eBay affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and make a qualifying purchase, LucyLP may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

External Links

LucyLP may link to external websites, including eBay, Facebook, TikTok, and other collector resources. External websites have their own privacy practices, and LucyLP is not responsible for their content or policies.

No Sale Of Personal Data

LucyLP does not sell personal data.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact lucy@lucylp.com.
