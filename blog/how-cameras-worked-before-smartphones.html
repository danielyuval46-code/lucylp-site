How Cameras Worked Before Smartphones

Story

Before smartphones, taking a picture was a little ceremony. Someone held a camera, checked the light, waited for everyone to stand still, and pressed a button without knowing exactly how the photo would look.

Cameras helped families save birthdays, trips, school days, and ordinary afternoons. They were not just machines. They were memory keepers.

Interesting Fact

Many families had to finish a roll of film and take it to a shop before they could see their photos. Waiting was part of the adventure.
